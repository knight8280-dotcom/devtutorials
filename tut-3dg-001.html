Course Overview

Comprehensive course on 3D game development covering modeling, texturing, lighting, and 3D-specific gameplay mechanics in Unreal Engine 5.

What You'll Learn

  • 3D Modeling Basics: Understanding meshes, polygons, and geometry
  • Material System: PBR materials, textures, and shader creation
  • Lighting Techniques: Dynamic lighting, Lumen, and light baking
  • 3D Character Setup: Rigging, skeletal meshes, and animations
  • Camera Control: Third-person, first-person camera systems
  • 3D Physics: Rigid bodies, constraints, and collision
  • Level Design: Blocking out and decorating 3D environments
  • Performance: LODs, culling, and optimization strategies

What's Included

Includes: 19 hours video, 3D asset pack, project files, PDF guide (140 pages), community access, lifetime updates

Prerequisites

  • Basic Unreal Engine knowledge recommended
  • Understanding of Blueprint fundamentals
