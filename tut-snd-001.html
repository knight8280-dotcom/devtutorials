Sound and Music Integration

Course Overview

Complete guide to audio implementation in games covering sound effects, music systems, audio mixing, and dynamic soundscapes using Unreal's audio tools.

What You'll Learn

  • Audio Import: Formats, compression, and asset management
  • Sound Cues: Creating complex audio with sound cues
  • 3D Audio: Spatial audio and attenuation settings
  • Music System: Dynamic music with layering and transitions
  • Audio Mixing: Volume, EQ, and sound classes
  • Ambient Sound: Environmental audio and reverb
  • Voice Integration: Dialogue and voice-over setup
  • Performance: Audio optimization and streaming

What's Included

Includes: 11 hours video, sound effect library, music tracks, project files, PDF guide (85 pages), community access

Prerequisites

  • Basic Unreal Engine knowledge recommended
  • Understanding of game development fundamentals
