General Questions

What is UE5 Academy?

UE5 Academy is a premium online learning platform specializing in Unreal Engine 5.6 training. We offer professional-quality courses created by industry veterans with 10+ years of AAA game development experience. Our courses cover everything from landscape creation to combat systems, all designed for serious game developers.

Who are these courses for?

Our courses are designed for:

  • Beginners: New to UE5 but want structured, professional training
  • Intermediate Developers: Know basics but want to master specific systems
  • Unity Developers: Switching to Unreal Engine from other engines
  • Students: Game design/development students building portfolio projects
  • Indie Developers: Solo devs or small teams building commercial games
  • Professional Developers: Industry pros wanting to level up specific skills

Do I need prior experience?

It depends on the course:

  • Blueprint Basics: No prior experience needed - starts from zero
  • Map Generation: Basic UE5 navigation helpful but not required
  • Character Creation: Some UE5 knowledge recommended
  • Combat System: Intermediate - should know Blueprints basics
  • Advanced Courses: Require solid understanding of UE5 fundamentals

Each course page lists specific prerequisites. If unsure, start with our Blueprint Visual Scripting course.

Technical Requirements

What are the system requirements?

For Unreal Engine 5.6:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 64-bit, or macOS 12.0+, or Linux
  • Processor: Quad-core Intel or AMD, 2.5 GHz or faster
  • RAM: 16GB minimum (32GB recommended)
  • Graphics: DirectX 11/12 compatible with 6GB+ VRAM (8GB+ recommended)
  • Storage: 100GB+ free space (SSD highly recommended)

Note: UE5 is demanding. A gaming-capable PC is recommended. Laptops work but may struggle with complex scenes.

Do I need to buy Unreal Engine?

No! Unreal Engine is completely free. Download it from Epic Games Launcher at no cost. Epic only charges a 5% royalty if your game earns over $1 million. For learning and most indie projects, it's 100% free.

Can I use course assets in my commercial games?

Yes, absolutely! All Blueprints, assets, and code included in our courses can be used in your commercial projects without additional licensing fees. We want you to succeed! Just don't resell our course materials themselves.
